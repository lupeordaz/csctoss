Using Redmine  

    

Redmine must capture the following:



  1. The task/bug/project you are working on.


  2. The estimated time of the task (your estimates are fine and will get better the more you use 
this feature)


  3. The estimated start date, and end date you (or your FO) is expecting.


  4. Percent complete


  5. Time tracking.  There is a button for "Log time" on each ticket.  Even if you don't put in 
specific updates, you should log how much time per day you work on a ticket
