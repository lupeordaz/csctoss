vi Tips & Tricks  


    
Sort rows in vi

  :%!sort

Turn line numbering on and off

  Turn line numbering on

    :set number

  Turn line numbering off

    :set nonumber

Display current line

    :num

Vi Indentation

  Turning automatic indentation on

    :set autoindent

  Setting the indentation level

    :set shiftwidth=4

    OR

    :1,$!sort

Substitution

  The folowing command:

    :%s/desktop/laptop/g 
  
  would replace each occurrence of "desktop" with "laptop" throughout the file; it works 
  just like the Linux sed command.


Search & Replace

  First occurrence on current line:      :s/OLD/NEW

  Globally (all) on current line:        :s/OLD/NEW/g 

  Between two lines #,#:                 :#,#s/OLD/NEW/g

  Every occurrence in file:              :%s/OLD/NEW/g

  Remove Windows end of line characters
  
  Use Ctl-v/Ctl-m method to create the '^M' character:

   :%s/^M//g
